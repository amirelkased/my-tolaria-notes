# Tolaria

Tolaria is a markdown-based, git-first, offline-first personal knowledge base. Built for people who want full control over their notes with zero lock-in.

It was created by [[luca-rossi]] to manage his own 10,000+ note knowledge base, then open-sourced for everyone.
